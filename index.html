3. Can I share my BagThat site on social media?
Yes. Share it anywhere you normally share content.

4. Do you make commission from my sales?
No. All commissions earned through your links go directly to you.

5. Is any customer data collected?
No. We do not collect or store customer or order information.

6. Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes. You can cancel at anytime as this is a monthly subscription. Once you cancel, you will have access to your site for the remainder of that current billing cycle and will not be charged after that. Please understand that there are no refunds.

7. Will this work if I don’t have an audience yet?
No. BagThatThangUp is designed for creators who already have followers, a group, or a list to send to their deals site. You still need traffic for any tool to generate commissions.
